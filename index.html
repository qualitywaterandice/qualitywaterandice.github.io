About our water

7-stage reverse osmosis

Our water is run through a 7-stage reverse osmosis system.

Our system includes several filtration stages, such as a sediment filter, activated carbon filter, and several reverse osmosis membranes.

This produces clean, delicious, filtered drinking water that is refreshing and pure, with no unpleasant aftertaste!

Alkaline water

Alkaline water is a refreshing and hydrating alternative to traditional tap or bottled water. We are happy to provide delicious, healthy alkaline water.

With a higher pH level than regular water, alkaline water can help to neutralize acidity in the body, leading to potential health benefits such as improved digestion and increased energy.

By incorporating alkaline water into your daily routine, you can support your body's natural pH balance and experience the benefits of optimal hydration!
